# Reference Links

## What

This file keeps deeper links separate from the main learning material.

## Why

The app should stay readable. References should support the module, not interrupt it.

## How To Use

Use a small `References` or `Further Reading` area at the bottom of future summary or scenario pages.

Keep links limited to 3-5 per page.
