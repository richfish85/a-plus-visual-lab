# Troubleshooting Mobile Devices

What

Mobile device troubleshooting covers phones and tablets that will not charge, will not connect, will not sync, or will not hold a stable connection to Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or a computer. The issue may be power, settings, account sync, or a bad cable.

Why

Mobile problems often look bigger than they are because the device is small and the user expects everything to "just work". A calm checklist helps the learner isolate the issue without jumping into resets too early.

How

Use this order:

- Check battery, charger, and cable.
- Check airplane mode, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and hotspot settings.
- Confirm the app or account is signed in.
- Try another cable, port, or charger.
- Restart the device and update if needed.
- Re-pair or re-enroll only after the basics.

Typical signs:

- Not charging or charging slowly.
- Wi-Fi drops or Bluetooth will not pair.
- Sync delays for mail, contacts, or files.
- App connection issues after an update.
- USB connection works for charging but not data.
