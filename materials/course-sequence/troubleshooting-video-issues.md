# Troubleshooting Video Issues

What

Video troubleshooting covers blank screens, flickering, poor playback, and display connection issues. The most common causes are cable problems, the wrong display mode, refresh-rate mismatch, or a graphics driver issue.

Why

Video faults are stressful because they block the whole interface, not just one app. Learners need a simple way to separate cable and monitor issues from driver and configuration issues.

How

Use a short checklist:

- Confirm the monitor has power.
- Check the cable and input source.
- Try another video port or cable.
- Reset or update the graphics driver.
- Check display mode and refresh rate.
- Test with another monitor if available.

Typical signs:

- Black screen at startup or after login.
- Flickering desktop or flickering taskbar.
- Output appears on the wrong display.
- Poor video playback or stuttering.
- External monitor not detected.
