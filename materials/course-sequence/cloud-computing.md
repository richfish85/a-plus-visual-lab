# Cloud Computing

## What

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services over the internet.

Those services can include servers, storage, networking, software, and other managed resources.

## Why

Learners need a simple cloud model because A+ questions often compare cloud services with local hardware and ask what the cloud is good for.

It also helps explain why a company might rent services instead of buying and maintaining everything itself.

## How

- Think of the cloud as rented computing power and storage.
- The provider owns and manages the remote infrastructure.
- The customer uses only the services it needs.
- Common service models are IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS.
- Common deployment models are public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud.

Examples:

- IaaS gives you more control over servers, storage, and networking.
- PaaS gives you a managed platform for building and deploying apps.
- SaaS gives you a finished application delivered as a service.
