# Cooling And Power

## What

This topic covers how a device stays powered and stays cool.

It includes power supplies, adapters, batteries, fans, heatsinks, and the basic idea that heat and power problems can look like many other faults.

## Why

Cooling and power issues are common and easy to misread.

If a learner jumps too quickly to a deep fix, they can miss the safer first check.

## How

- Check whether the device powers on.
- Confirm whether the charger, outlet, or battery path is working.
- Look for fan noise, blocked vents, or obvious heat symptoms.
- Treat the simplest visible cause first.
