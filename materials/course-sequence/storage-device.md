# Storage Device

## What

Storage devices keep files and installed software after power is off.

In the course sequence, this covers SSDs, HDDs, SATA, and M.2-level concepts at the learner-friendly service level.

## Why

Storage is a common exam and lab topic because it connects physical parts to symptoms like slow startup, missing files, and boot problems.

## How

- Compare shape and connector style.
- Explain where the device sits in the system.
- Tie the part to common symptoms.
- Keep the lesson focused on recognition and first-step troubleshooting.
