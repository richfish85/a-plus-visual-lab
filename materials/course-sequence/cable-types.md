# Cable Types

## What

Cable types are the physical connectors and leads used to move data, video, and power.

For this project, the learner is mainly learning to tell common shapes apart: RJ45, USB-A, USB-C, HDMI, and Thunderbolt over USB-C.

## Why

Cable recognition is one of the fastest wins in A+ study.

If the learner can identify the connector shape quickly, the rest of the troubleshooting flow becomes easier.

## How

- Learn the shape first.
- Learn the purpose second.
- Match the cable to the port before worrying about speed tiers or marketing labels.

Notes:

- USB Type-C is the connector shape, not the same thing as every protocol that can use it.
- Thunderbolt uses the USB-C shape on many devices, but not every USB-C port is Thunderbolt.
- HDMI is the standard display connector in this set.
