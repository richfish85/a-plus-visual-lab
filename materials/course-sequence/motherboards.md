# Motherboards

## What

A motherboard is the main circuit board in a PC or laptop.

It is the place where the processor, memory, storage connectors, power delivery, firmware, and expansion paths meet.

## Why

Motherboards matter because many A+ questions are really about finding where a part connects or what role a slot plays.

Once the learner can read the board, they can reason about upgrades and common faults more confidently.

## How

- Start with board layout.
- Find the CPU socket or soldered CPU area.
- Find the memory slots.
- Find storage connectors, power connectors, and front-panel or port headers.
